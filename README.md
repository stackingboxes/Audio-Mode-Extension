# Audio Mode Extension
📺 YouTube CPU Saver & Smart Quality Manager
A lightweight browser extension designed to optimize your computer's performance by intelligently managing YouTube video quality.

If you are running on a lower-end computer, an older laptop, or just want to reduce unnecessary CPU load and bandwidth, this tool does the heavy lifting for you. It recognizes that you don't need 1080p video rendering to listen to a song, and automatically adjusts your settings to save system resources.

## Key Features

🎵 Smart Music Detection
Automatically detects if a video is in the "Music" category and instantly drops the resolution to the lowest available quality (e.g., 144p).

⚙️ Preferred Default Quality
For all non-music videos (gaming, tutorials, documentaries), the extension automatically forces your pre-selected preferred resolution (e.g., 1080p or 720p).

🔄 One-Click Quick Toggle
Adds a custom, native-looking button directly into the YouTube player control bar (next to Captions and Settings). Click it to instantly flip between the lowest quality and your preferred quality.

🔋 Performance First
Drastically reduces CPU and GPU video decoding overhead, preventing lag, overheating, and battery drain on lower-end hardware.

## 🤔 Why use this?

Video decoding is a highly resource-intensive process. When you leave a YouTube music playlist running in the background, your browser is still working hard to render high-definition video frames that you aren't even looking at.

This extension serves as a "set it and forget it" solution. It ensures your computer only works hard when there is actual visual content you want to watch, keeping your system fast and responsive.

## 🚀 Installation

For Google Chrome & Microsoft Edge (Developer Mode)
Download the latest release from the Releases tab or clone this repository.

1. Unzip the downloaded file to a dedicated folder on your computer.

2. Open your browser and navigate to the extensions page:

3. Chrome: Type chrome://extensions/ in the URL bar.

   Edge: Type edge://extensions/ in the URL bar.

4. Enable Developer mode (usually a toggle switch in the top right corner).

5. Click the Load unpacked button and select the folder where you unzipped the extension.

The extension is now installed! 
Before it works, click on the extension, click on options and follow the instructions for (basically) unlimited uses! 

🛠️ Usage Guide
Set your preference: Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar to open the options menu and set your Preferred Quality (e.g., 1080p).

Watch a video: Navigate to any standard YouTube video. It will automatically load in your preferred quality.

Listen to music: Navigate to a music video. Notice the quality automatically drops to 144p, saving your CPU.

Use the toggle: Need to quickly see the video for a music track, or want to drop the quality of a podcast? Just hit the new Quick Toggle button in the YouTube video player to instantly swap between 144p and your preferred quality.

## FAQ
### Why not just use YouTube Music?
#### 1. Unmatched Content Library 
While YouTube Music is great for official releases, standard YouTube is home to millions of unofficial tracks, hour-long DJ mixes, live performances, indie covers, and specific game soundtracks that are often unavailable on official streaming platforms. This extension ensures you can listen to anything on YouTube without paying a performance penalty.

#### 2. Seamless Browsing Flow   
Users constantly switch between content types. You might watch a gaming tutorial, listen to a music track, and then watch a documentary, all within the same tab or playlist. Switching back and forth between youtube.com and music.youtube.com breaks that workflow. This extension allows you to stay on the main platform while invisibly managing your system resources in the background.

#### 3. Visuals on Demand (The Quick Toggle Advantage)
YouTube Music focuses primarily on audio. But what if a music video has incredible choreography or visuals you actually want to see for a moment? With this extension, you stay on the main video platform. If you want to watch the video, simply hit the Quick Toggle button to jump to high quality, watch the scene, and click it again to drop back to an optimized background state.

#### 4. Tab and Memory Management
For lower-end computers, keeping browser tabs to a minimum is just as important as reducing CPU load. Forcing users to keep a dedicated YouTube Music tab open alongside their regular YouTube tab consumes extra RAM and browser resources. Keeping everything centralized in one optimized tab is the most efficient solution for older hardware.

### Why is this video's quality different than it should be?
First, ensure that any adblockers are disabled or not interfering with the extension as this can sometimes cause problems. If the issue persists, it is likely due to how the video was categorized by its creator.

This extension relies on the official YouTube category set by the uploader to trigger the quality change. If a creator uploads a standard video but categorizes it as "Music" (or vice versa), the extension will apply the corresponding settings for that category.
